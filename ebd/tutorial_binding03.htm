Binding technique : Interface 03


By now you should have an idea on how this binding technique works. This time we try to bind two rectangular figures with rounded corners using 2 inside curves.


Figure 01


Figure 02


Figure 3 illustrates the map of this interface. The design uses 2 inside curves to bind the 2 rectangular figures. Figure 4 shows the position of the circular selection used to cut and create one of the curves. I positioned the left end of the lower curve near the lower rounded edge to create the illusion that the rounded corner of the first rectangular figure extends to the next rectangle.
