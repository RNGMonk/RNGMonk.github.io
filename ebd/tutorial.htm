The tutorials below feature my own ways of creating my effects in photoshop® and all of them are my own variations and they may be completely diffrent from the tutorials you have seen from other sites. Im sure you'll find them helpful in a way. I've divided them into their own categories so you won't have trouble looking for the tutorials you want.

Notice:
The current tutorials I have work for version 4 of photoshop®. I don't know if they would work properly with the new version. I've also used some terms which might appear new to you especially for if you're a newbie and I would suggest that you use help file or your manual. I've also included the PSD files that I used for the tutorials and made them available for download. They should help if you have trouble seeing the layering of the images or effects.
