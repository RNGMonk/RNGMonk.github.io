Dont't be deceived by Anya's "whatever" attitude for this young lady is vibrant, fun-loving and even outrageous person with a good head on her shoulders. She handles herself very and appreciates even the simplest gestures. Although not physically strong (a euphemism for a weakling), she is very strong emotionally so her close friends depend on her listening ear and a shoulder to cry on.
