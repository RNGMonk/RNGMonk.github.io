To do an action such as Andrew Jumps up and down you would type: /me jumps up and down.

To change your nickname: /nick NewName
(NewName is the new nick name you wish to have)

example: /nick Andrew would change your name to Andrew
To join a new room: /join #roomname The name of the room will be where roomname is such as /join #FxZone you also always need to have the # otherwise you won't join a room.

To go into private chat with someone you can double click on his or her name from the nickname list to the right of the screen or you can type /query NickName where nickname is the name of the person you wish to talk to.

example:/query Andrew will take me into private with the user Andrew

To do colours in the chat you to ctrl+k and a number from 0-15. In mirc a panel will come up (shown below) that will show the values of the numbers 0-15 so that you know which colour you are getting.

 In our applet we have a colour panel to the left of the area in which you type so that you can do colours easily. You click the colour once to use it one time, click it twice to use it all the time and click it twice again to go back to your normal colour font.
