Register your nick name:
/msg nickserv register password email
Make sure you put a real email address cause if you forget your password they will email it to the email, which you used to register your nick with.

Identify to Nickserv:
/msg nickserv identify password
This identifies you to nickserv so that you can get channel op status if you have it in a certain channel as well as check your memos with memoserv.

Actions:
To do an action such as Andrew Jumps up and down you would type /me jumps up and down.

Changing your nick:
To change your nickname: /nick NewName (NewName is the new nick name you wish to have)

Private Chatting:
To go into private chat with someone you can double click on his or her name from the nickname list to the right of the screen or you can type /query NickName where nickname is the name of the person you wish to talk to.
