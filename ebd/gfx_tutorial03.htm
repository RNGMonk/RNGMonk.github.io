Rounded Edges
To start off, make a perfect cicrular selection and fill the selection with the desired color.

Using the marquee tool, make a rectangular selection at half of the circle. With the selection still loaded select the move tool and hold the SHIFT KEY then drag horizontally to one end.

Now make a selection on the lower half of the two semi-circles using the marquee tool.

With the selection still loaded select the move tool and hold the SHIFT KEY then drag vertically to the lower end.

Make a rectangular selection on the inside area of the 4 corners and fill that selection. You might want to use guides when doing this part of the tutorial.

Repeat the process above to completely fill and make a square or rectangle with the rounded edges.
